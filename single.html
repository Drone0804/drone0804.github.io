What is This Technology?

What is Brain Computer Interface (BCI)? BCIs enable it's users to interact with computers in the means of brain activity. For example, if someone lost one of their limbs and replaced it with a prosthetic, the BCI machine would send signals from your brain to the prosthetic.

It is imporatant to use this technology if you have a prosthetic because your brain normally sends signals around your body but when you lose a limb the brain won't be able to send signal for your prosthetic to work in the way you want it to.

Applications

Neuralink is a company that created a tecchnology called Nueralace. This campany was founded by Elon Musk in 2016, and was first publicly reported in 2017. The company's headquarters takes place in San Francisco. Since the company has been founded, they have hired several high-profile neuroscientists. By 2019, the company's fund was $158 million and they had 90 employees. In July 2019, Neuralink was working on a device capable of implanting very thin threads into the brain, demonstrated a system that read information from a lab rat through 1,500 electrodes and said would start experiments with humans in 2020. In summary, this technology is like a mesh that fitss at the surface of your brain. Using this technology, you can download or upload data from computers. You can become smarter than a computer!

There is another BCI application called Electroencephalography (EEG). EEG is a test used to find problems related to electrical activity of the brain. An EEG tracks and records brain wave patterns. Small metal discs with thin wires (electrodes) are placed on the scalp, and then send signals to a computer to record the results. There is another BCI application similar to an EEG called Electrocoricography (ECOG). ECOG is an invasive method that uses electrodes directly on the exposed surface of the brain to record electrical activity from the cerebral cortex. ECOG can be used to help people with epilepsy by creating a map of the brain of the person to identify exactly where the seizures are occurring. ECOG has greater precision and sensitivity than EEG scalp recording as it's spatial resolution is higher.

Neural Dust is a new BCI technology that is roughly 3 millimeters long. This is more efficient and alternitave to EEGs. This small piece of technology is placed inside your brain and can also control prosthetics. Neural dust converts ultrasound vibrations into electricity and then transmits information to a receiver located outside the body.

This technology is really small compared to other technologies and the goal is to get the Neural Dust to be 50 microns wide. This is half of the width of an average human's hair. This can be very useful because if someone is about to have a seizure, the technology will send a signal to the device outside the body so you will know when you're about to have a seizure.
